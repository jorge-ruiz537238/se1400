Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

This site will contain new and important news in the soccer/football world. It will match reports, player interviews, and analysis of recent games and tournaments.

Where will you get all of the content for your site?

I will get my content for this site using pictures from Google and recent news from reporters while making sure to give them credit.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Not everyone has access to social media or can afford streaming and cable services to keep up with the latest soccer news. This site will provide free access to important news in the soccer world and people should care because it is a free accesible way to stay connected with their interests.

Why should a site be built for this?

Soccer is the most popular sport in the world. Many people don't have access to social media or cable services so this website will provide a free and accessible way for people to stay connected with their interests.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to be informed with the current and latest news about the soccer world. Their is nothing more to it other than a way for people to stay connected with their interests and passions.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of this site is simply for a way for people to stay informed with current soccer news and events.

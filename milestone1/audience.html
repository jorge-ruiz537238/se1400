Audience Analysis:

Who is the target audience for your site?

The target audience for my site is soccer enthusiasts of all ages who are looking for free and accessible news about the sport. This includes casual fans, dedicated followers, and individuals who may not have access to paid subscriptions or cable services.

What are their needs and goals?

Their needs include staying informed about the latest soccer news, match results, player updates, and tournament analyses. Their goals are to remain connected with their favorite teams and players without incurring additional costs.

What are their demographics?

The demographics of the target audience are diverse, spanning various age groups and geographic locations. Soccer is a global sport, so the audience may include individuals from different countries, cultures, and socioeconomic backgrounds. More young audiences may access the site more frequently due to their familiarity with digital platforms and their interests with new updates.
